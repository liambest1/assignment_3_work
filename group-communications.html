Groups processes and communications

Communication has been a strong point for Team Exodus. We have had two weekly team meetings each week, often between 45 minutes and 1 hour in length. Each meeting is recorded. We also have one person in each meeting taking minutes, which is saved in a minutes folder on Sharepoint, with the link of the recording. If anyone misses a meeting, they are required to let the rest of the team know with as much notice as possible, then they must watch the recording, read the minutes and message the group and make sure they are back on track. We kept very active in the Microsoft Teams chat as well, with all six members often communication at once every day. Delegation of tasks was another area of strength, with each member always having a clear view of the tasks the were required to contribute towards.
